BMESC Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-22

BMESC is a companion app for nearby BMESC hardware. The first App Store release uses Bluetooth to discover, connect to, and display local device status.

Data Collection

The app does not require an account, does not collect personal information, and does not upload telemetry to a cloud service in this release. Device telemetry is used locally on the phone to show connection state, speed, battery, mileage, temperature, and fault status.

Bluetooth

Bluetooth access is used only to scan for and connect to nearby compatible BMESC devices.

Fault Logs

The app may store local fault log entries on the device so users can review recent safety messages. These logs remain on the user's device unless the user chooses to share them with support.

Support

If users contact support, they may choose to provide diagnostic details such as device name, node ID, fault code, screenshots, and the time of the issue.

BMESC 隐私政策

生效日期：2026-06-22

BMESC 是配套 BMESC 硬件使用的移动端应用。首个 App Store 版本通过蓝牙发现、连接附近设备，并在本机显示设备状态。

数据收集

本版本不需要账号，不收集个人信息，也不会将遥测数据上传到云端。设备遥测仅用于在手机本地显示连接状态、速度、电量、里程、温度和故障状态。

蓝牙

蓝牙权限仅用于扫描并连接附近兼容的 BMESC 设备。

故障日志

App 可能在本机保存故障日志，方便用户查看近期安全提示。除非用户主动分享给支持人员，这些日志不会离开用户设备。

支持联系

用户联系支持时，可自行选择提供设备名称、节点 ID、故障代码、截图和问题发生时间等诊断信息。
